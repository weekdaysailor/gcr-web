The carbon reward (XCR) is a proposed sovereign-supported financial instrument issued for verified climate mitigation and removal. It aims to mobilize private capital by pairing long-duration contracts with a defended price floor overseen by a future central bank alliance.

Not an offset: XCR is tradable but not a carbon credit and cannot be used to offset emissions. It rewards real-world mitigation at the source and atmospheric removals.

  • Bankable asset: Denominated as “XCR”, designed for FX markets, with proposed central bank alliance support.
  • Debt-free issuance: Created without adding fiscal burden; price support defends long-term credibility.
  • Performance-based: Contracts include milestones, verification, and clawbacks to keep incentives tight.
