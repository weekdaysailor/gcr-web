# Our Logo

<div style="text-align: center; margin: 2rem 0;">
  <img src="/images/gcr-logo.png" alt="Global Carbon Reward logo" style="max-width: 300px; width: 100%;">
</div>

The official Global Carbon Reward emblem is provided above. You can reference it consistently across decks, presentations, and articles. The logo contains four major symbolic elements designed to represent the organization's climate and economic mission.

## Four Key Elements

<div class="summary-grid">
  <div class="summary-card">
    <img src="/images/logo-icon-01.png" alt="The Letter C" style="width: 100px; margin: 0 auto 1rem; display: block;">
    <h3>1. The Letter "C"</h3>
    <p>Represents elemental carbon and symbolizes greenhouse gas mitigation rather than pollution. The blue color signifies "the Earth's atmosphere during the day."</p>
  </div>

  <div class="summary-card">
    <img src="/images/logo-icon-02.png" alt="The Reboot Symbol" style="width: 100px; margin: 0 auto 1rem; display: block;">
    <h3>2. The Reboot Symbol</h3>
    <p>The "C" combines with a line to form the iconic reboot symbol found on computer power switches. This represents the policy's goal to "reboot the world economy for stabilising the climate and protecting the planetary ecosystem."</p>
  </div>

  <div class="summary-card">
    <img src="/images/logo-icon-03.png" alt="Thermometer Design" style="width: 100px; margin: 0 auto 1rem; display: block;">
    <h3>3. Thermometer Design</h3>
    <p>The reboot symbol's line features a distinctive bulge representing the policy's target of remaining "below 1.5 to 2.0Â°C of global warming as proposed in the 2015 Paris Climate Agreement." The curved line resembles a thermometer reflecting Earth's surface temperature concerns.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="summary-card">
    <img src="/images/logo-icon-04.png" alt="Seventeen Leaves" style="width: 100px; margin: 0 auto 1rem; display: block;">
    <h3>4. Seventeen Leaves</h3>
    <p>The logo includes 17 leaves representing the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The leaves feature a "red-green-blue colour wheel over a 360 degree rotation" symbolizing "the flow of time, the diversity of species...and the diversity of human cultures."</p>
  </div>
</div>

## UN Sustainable Development Goals

<div style="text-align: center; margin: 2rem 0;">
  <img src="/images/sdgs-poster.png" alt="United Nations 17 Sustainable Development Goals" style="max-width: 600px; width: 100%;">
</div>

The 17 leaves in our logo directly correspond to the United Nations' 17 Sustainable Development Goals, representing our commitment to holistic climate action that addresses economic, social, and environmental sustainability.
