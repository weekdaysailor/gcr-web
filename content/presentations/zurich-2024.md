# Carbon as Currency?

**Event:** GreenBuzz | Zurich
**Date:** September 18, 2024
**Location:** Zurich, Switzerland

## Description

The presentation explored economic system transformation and climate solutions. Drawing inspiration from "The Ministry for the Future," the event examined "the intersection of blockchain, currency, and carbon" to assess feasibility of greening economics amid rising global emissions despite international climate pledges.
