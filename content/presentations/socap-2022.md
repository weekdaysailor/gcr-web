# Do we need a second economy for carbon? How to think like a plant

**Event:** SOCAP 2022
**Date:** October 18, 2022
**Location:** YBCA, San Francisco, California

## Context

SOCAP convenes a diverse impact investing community focused on interconnected economic, social, and environmental systems. The organization emphasizes that "the climate crisis waits for no one and touches everyone."

## Key Participants

- **Moderator:** Alexis Bunten (Co-Director, Bioneers Indigeneity Program)
- **Speaker:** Dr. Delton Chen (Global Carbon Reward), described as a "civil engineer, geo-hydrologist" who originated the carbon currency concept referenced in the novel "The Ministry for the Future"

## Main Theme

The presentation explores whether a parallel economic system centered on carbon mechanisms could support climate action and regenerative finance.
