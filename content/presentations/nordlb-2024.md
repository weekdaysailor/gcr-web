# Addressing the Climate Crisis with Carrots & Sticks

**Event:** NORD/LB Capital Markets Conference
**Date:** August 27-28, 2024
**Location:** Herrenhausen Palace, Hanover, Germany
**Theme:** "Mission: (Im)Possible" addressing climate change challenges

## Venue Information

The conference was hosted at Herrenhausen Palace, the former residence of Hanover's Electors and Kings. Built between 1819-1821, it was reconstructed after 1943 destruction and reopened in 2013 as a conference center and museum.

## Key Speakers

- **Tim Malloch** - Senior Associate, CMS Cameron McKenna Nabarro Olswang LLP (introduction)
- **Dr. Delton Chen** - Project Director and Founder, Global Carbon Reward (keynote speaker)
- **Jan Berger** - Participated in fireside conversation

## Presentation Topic

"Addressing the Climate Crisis with Carrots & Sticks: Rethinking International Finance for Accelerating Low-Carbon Projects"

## Related Publication

Featured in "Futures of the European Capital Market" report by Themis Foresight and NORD/LB, analyzing the European capital market's role in green transition and energy efficiency.
