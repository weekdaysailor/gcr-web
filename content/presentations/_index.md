## Select Presentations

Dr. Delton Chen and the Global Carbon Reward team have presented the carbon reward policy at major conferences, universities, and institutions around the world. These presentations demonstrate the policy's capacity to mobilize climate action while supporting healthy ecosystems and vibrant communities.

### Featured Events

The GCR policy has been showcased at prestigious venues including climate conferences (VERGE, COP28), leading universities (Harvard, Lund, Amsterdam, Bard), financial institutions (Bank of England, NORD/LB), and international summits (Ecocity World Summit, SOCAP).
