## Global Carbon Reward for Policymakers

The Global Carbon Reward supplies policymakers with a treaty-ready incentive that respects sovereignty, avoids new taxes or debt, and mobilizes multilateral finance at systemic scale.

      <li>Independent verification authority manages MRV + clawbacks</li>
    </ul>

  <article class="pathway-card">

      <li>Liquidity operations minimize FX volatility</li>
    </ul>
