Donate to the Global Carbon Reward

Support the Global Carbon Reward initiative through tax-deductible contributions.

Fuel the Global Carbon Reward initiative

Your contribution accelerates policy outreach, demonstration projects, and the systems design work required to bring the Global Carbon Reward to governments and markets worldwide.

Donate with PayPal

Use the secure PayPal button below to make a one-time or recurring contribution. Donations are processed by Inquiring Systems Inc. (ISI), our 501(c)(3) fiscal sponsor.

Prefer to send funds manually? Use PayPal’s “Send money” option and direct your contribution to globalcarbon@inquiringsystems.org.

Need assistance? Call +1 (707) 939-3900 for help with donations or receipts.

Donate with a credit card (without a PayPal account)

  1. Visit paypal.com.
  2. Select Send & Request → Send money.
  3. Enter globalcarbon@inquiringsystems.org as the recipient and choose your preferred amount.

Need help?

  • Call +1 (707) 939-3900 during Sonoma working hours for assistance with donations or receipts.
  • PayPal charges a 2.9% fee plus $0.30 per transaction. To avoid these charges, consider sending a check or contact us for alternative instructions.

Tax status and receipts

  • Inquiring Systems Inc. (ISI) is a registered 501(c)(3) organization. All contributions are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
  • Receipts will reference ISI and the Global Carbon Reward initiative.

Questions about partnerships or major gifts?

Please reach out to our team with details about your organization, funding timeline, and focus area. We’ll coordinate a briefing to align your support with the highest-impact policy and demonstration needs.
