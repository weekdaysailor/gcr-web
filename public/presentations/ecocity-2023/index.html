EcoCity World Summit 2023: Towards a Decarbonised Economy

Event: EcoCity World Summit 2023 Date: June 6, 2023 Location: Barbican Centre, London

Description

Dr. Delton Chen presented a new economic development framework called “optimal growth” (later rebranded as “balanced growth in carbon” for clarity). This approach combines innovative climate policy with a carbon currency mechanism designed to encourage collaborative efforts between governments and financial institutions in pursuing Paris climate accord targets.

Key Participants

Chair: Carol Lewis, Property Editor at The Times and The Sunday Times

Speaker & Presenter: Dr. Delton Chen, Project Director & Founder of Global Carbon Reward

Speakers:

  • Catherine McGuinness (Connected Places Catapult, City of London Corporation)
  • Lucy Yu (CEO Centre for Net Zero, Octopus Energy)

Presentation Focus

The discussion examined green finance initiatives, carbon mitigation strategies, and net-zero implementation challenges and opportunities for governments, society, and urban centers.
