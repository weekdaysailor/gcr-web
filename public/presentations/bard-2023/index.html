Why 'Carbon Rewards' Should be Adopted to Resolve the Climate Crisis

Why ‘Carbon Rewards’ Should be Adopted to Resolve the Climate Crisis

Event: MBA Sustainability, Residency Weekend Date: October 7, 2023 Location: Bard College, New York

Description

Dr. Delton Chen presented an economic theory and climate policy concept featuring a “carbon currency” backed by central banks to finance global economic decarbonization. The presentation addressed how this market tool could improve international cooperation and resolve market failures through a mechanism called “carbon reward,” which includes ecosystem and community regeneration components.

Key Participants

Chair: Janice Shade, Adjunct Professor of Finance & Accounting at Bard College (systems entrepreneur and financial innovator)

Speaker: Dr. Delton Chen, Project Director & Founder of Global Carbon Reward (civil engineer, geo-hydrologist, originator of the “carbon currency” concept featured in the novel The Ministry for the Future)
