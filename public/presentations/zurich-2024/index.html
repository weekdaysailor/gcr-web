Carbon as Currency?

Carbon as Currency?

Event: GreenBuzz | Zurich Date: September 18, 2024 Location: Zurich, Switzerland

Description

The presentation explored economic system transformation and climate solutions. Drawing inspiration from “The Ministry for the Future,” the event examined “the intersection of blockchain, currency, and carbon” to assess feasibility of greening economics amid rising global emissions despite international climate pledges.

Panel Composition

Moderator:

  • Vera Hauser (Institute for Global Negotiation & University of St. Gallen researcher)

Speakers:

  • Prof. Regina Betz (ZHAW Center for Energy and Environment)
  • Patrick Hofstetter (WWF Schweiz Climate & Energy Policy)
  • Joe Pretorius (Toco AG Co-Founder)
  • Dr. Delton Chen (Global Carbon Reward Initiative Founder)
