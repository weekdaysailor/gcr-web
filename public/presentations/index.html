Select Presentations

Global Carbon Reward presentations at conferences and universities worldwide

Select Presentations

Dr. Delton Chen and the Global Carbon Reward team have presented the carbon reward policy at major conferences, universities, and institutions around the world. These presentations demonstrate the policy’s capacity to mobilize climate action while supporting healthy ecosystems and vibrant communities.

Featured Events

The GCR policy has been showcased at prestigious venues including climate conferences (VERGE, COP28), leading universities (Harvard, Lund, Amsterdam, Bard), financial institutions (Bank of England, NORD/LB), and international summits (Ecocity World Summit, SOCAP).

Overview

The presentation explored the concept of “carbon coin,” a theoretical new currency backed by central banks that organizations could earn by reducing or eliminating emissions. Lund 2023

What can be done to resolve the climate crisis?

What can be done to resolve the climate crisis?

Event: Lunch Seminar at Lund University Date: March 22, 2023 Location: International Institute for Industrial Environmental Economics (IIIEE), Lund University

Speaker

Delton Chen, Ph.D. - Founder & Project Director for Global Carbon Reward

Coordinator

Desirée Pettersson - Communications Specialist

Key Content Summary

The presentation introduces the Global Carbon Reward policy, developed over nine years. Chen discusses:

  • A “carbon pricing matrix” as a conceptual framework
  • Introduction of “systemic externality” - societal lock-in preventing conventional climate policies from being applied effectively
  • The need to price social costs and systemic risks separately using a “carrot and stick” approach
  • A proposed “parallel economy” and “carbon currency” designed to remove atmospheric GHGs and strategically reduce emissions

Organization

Global Carbon Reward operates under fiscal sponsorship by Inquiring Systems Inc., a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

COP28 2023

Global Carbon Reward at COP28 UAE

COP28 UAE | Global Carbon Reward

Event: COP28 UAE Date: November 30 - December 12, 2023 Location: Dubai, UAE

Overview

The Global Carbon Reward (GCR) initiative presented at three separate COP28 side events. The organization positioned their policy as addressing a critical gap in the COP28 climate roadmap by providing a scalable financial mechanism for clean energy transitions and atmospheric carbon removal.

Key Message

The page emphasizes that while COP28 called for transitioning away from fossil fuels, it lacked comprehensive financing solutions. The GCR framework proposes a novel reward system for mitigated carbon that incorporates central banking mandates while avoiding problematic offsetting practices.
