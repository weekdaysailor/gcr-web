Global Carbon Reward at COP28 UAE

COP28 UAE | Global Carbon Reward

Event: COP28 UAE Date: November 30 - December 12, 2023 Location: Dubai, UAE

Overview

The Global Carbon Reward (GCR) initiative presented at three separate COP28 side events. The organization positioned their policy as addressing a critical gap in the COP28 climate roadmap by providing a scalable financial mechanism for clean energy transitions and atmospheric carbon removal.

Key Message

The page emphasizes that while COP28 called for transitioning away from fossil fuels, it lacked comprehensive financing solutions. The GCR framework proposes a novel reward system for mitigated carbon that incorporates central banking mandates while avoiding problematic offsetting practices.

Presentations Offered

  1. UNCC Global Innovation Hub - 10-minute presentation with slide deck (December 10, 2023)
  2. Bhutan Pavilion - 10-minute interview format
  3. Bhutan Pavilion - 1-hour discussion with Q&A session

Core Objectives

The policy aims to support three interconnected outcomes: “healthy ecosystems, vibrant communities, and reliable clean energy.”
