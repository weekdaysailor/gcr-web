Hong Kong 2022

Carbon Quantitative Easing - Club Breakfast

Carbon Quantitative Easing - Club Breakfast

Event: FCC Hong Kong Club Breakfast Date: December 15, 2022, 8:30 AM – 10:00 AM Location: Foreign Correspondents Club, Hong Kong (Dining Room)

Key Details

Speaker: Dr. Delton Chen, Global Carbon Reward

  • Civil engineer and geo-hydrologist specializing in digital currency policy
  • Originated the “carbon currency” concept featured in the novel The Ministry for the Future

Moderator: Joe Pan, FCC Board of Governors Member

  • Blockchain and Web3 journalist (Chinese and English)
  • Co-founder of Carbon Neutral Club
  • Focuses on linking ESG metrics to digital assets

Content Highlights

The presentation centered on Carbon Quantitative Easing (CQE), a financial mechanism designed to address climate challenges through new digital currency approaches. The event positioned this as a solution for “gridlock to a regenerative financial system.”

This breakfast forum brought together financial, technology, and climate-focused professionals to discuss innovative economic approaches to carbon management and environmental sustainability.
