2025 Climate Shift Policy Paper

The Global Carbon Reward policy framework is detailed in our latest research paper, available for download and citation.

Featured Event: The Economist Sustainability Week

Dates: March 10-12, 2025 Location: Convene Sancroft, London Event: The Economist’s 10th Annual Sustainability Week

Panel Discussion: “Grilling Economic Models”

The policy paper was presented alongside other perspectives on sustainable economics:

Panelists

Delton Chen (Global Carbon Reward founder) Presented ideas around carbon-backed digital assets and “balanced growth in carbon” for climate risk management.

Sue Miller (Stop Ecocide International) Discussed making ecocide an international crime as an accountability mechanism.

Jon Alexander (New Citizenship Project co-founder) Advocated for a “Citizen Future” prioritizing resilience and relational wellbeing over growth metrics.

Download the Policy Paper

The 2025 policy paper is available for download and includes detailed information about:

  • Carbon reward mechanism design
  • Economic modeling and implementation
  • Central bank integration
  • Global governance architecture
  • Case studies and demonstrations

Citation: Chen, D.B. (2025). Carbon Reward Policy: An Economic Framework for Responding to Climate Damages & Systemic Risks. DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.17341212
