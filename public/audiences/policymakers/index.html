For Policymakers

Legislative framework, international cooperation, and policy demonstrations

Global Carbon Reward for Policymakers

The Global Carbon Reward supplies policymakers with a treaty-ready incentive that respects sovereignty, avoids new taxes or debt, and mobilizes multilateral finance at systemic scale.

Policy Pillars

Policy Pillar

Treaty-Ready Architecture

The carbon reward scales from voluntary climate clubs to a multilateral treaty framework.

  • International Carbon Exchange Authority oversees rules and MRV
  • Countries choose demonstration scale and timing
  • Risk Cost of Carbon (RCC) coordination aligns monetary and climate goals

Policy Pillar

Central Bank Alliance

Monetary authorities defend the XCR price floor via CQE without issuing offsets or new government debt.

  • Mandates expand to climate risk management
  • Interventions only occur when spot prices test the floor
  • Supports financial stability during transition

Policy Pillar

Preventive Insurance Principle

Pairs polluter-pays with positive incentives, avoiding political friction around new carbon taxes.

  • No direct fiscal cost to treasuries or taxpayers
  • Rewards actual mitigation outcomes, not offsets
  • Encourages broad stakeholder coalitions

Policy Pillar

Transparent Verification

100-year durability contracts with independent MRV and clawbacks ensure accountability.

  • Reward Channel 3 ranks community + ecosystem co-benefits
  • Satellite and ground data feed public registries
  • Supports SDGs and equity metrics

Key Advantages

Advantage

Resolve Gridlock

Transforms cost-sharing disputes into market participation.

  • Countries contribute by GDP/emissions share
  • Reward funding accessible to all verified mitigation providers
  • Market pricing replaces tense burden negotiations

Advantage

Enable Ambition

Supports Paris-aligned pathways with science-based Risk Cost of Carbon (RCC) targets.

  • Mobilizes >US$3T annually without new taxes
  • Reward multipliers link SDGs and climate metrics
  • Supports national just-transition strategies

Advantage

Avoid Political Obstacles

Positive incentives sidestep carbon tax fatigue and debt concerns.

  • Central banks issue rewards, not treasuries
  • No requirement for offsets or compliance markets
  • Stimulates jobs + infrastructure while delivering mitigation

Advantage

Strengthen Equity

Reward Channel 3 delivers measurable co-benefit dividends.

  • Community wellbeing scores inform payouts
  • Ecosystem indicators built into contracts
  • Supports climate justice narratives and finance

Governance Architecture

Multilateral Structure

  • Climate clubs pilot the framework before treaty adoption
  • International treaty establishes principles + Risk Cost of Carbon (RCC) guidance
  • Independent verification authority manages MRV + clawbacks

Central Bank Role

  • Mandates add climate risk alongside price stability
  • CQE interventions defend the XCR price floor
  • Liquidity operations minimize FX volatility

Reward Channels

  • Channel 1: Carbon dioxide removal
  • Channel 2: Conventional mitigation
  • Channel 3: Community & ecosystem dividends
