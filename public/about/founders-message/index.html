Founder's Message

A message from Dr. Delton Chen, founder of the Global Carbon Reward Initiative

Founder’s Message

“The Global Carbon Reward is a citizen-led initiative to protect the climate, communities and ecosystems.” — Dr. Delton Chen

Welcome

Dr. Delton Chen founded the Global Carbon Reward Initiative based on using a carbon reward (XCR) to mobilize climate action globally. According to the page, this instrument serves multiple purposes: economic stimulus, ecosystem service payments, and long-term investment. Crucially, the reward market differs from existing carbon markets because it avoids carbon offsetting entirely.

Our Mission

The organization aims to communicate a fresh narrative about resolving climate challenges through the Global Carbon Reward mechanism, actively seeking official partners and sponsors for demonstration projects.

Important Update

In October 2025, the organization replaced the terminology “carbon currency” (XCC) with “carbon reward” (XCR) for improved scientific clarity.
