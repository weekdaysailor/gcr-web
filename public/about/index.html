About the Global Carbon Reward

The Global Carbon Reward is a comprehensive climate policy framework designed to mobilize unprecedented levels of finance for climate mitigation while supporting healthy ecosystems, vibrant communities, and reliable clean energy.

Our Approach

The Global Carbon Reward Initiative is a citizen-led effort to protect the climate, communities, and ecosystems through a novel financial mechanism called the carbon reward (XCR). This instrument serves multiple purposes: economic stimulus, ecosystem service payments, and long-term investment. Crucially, the reward market differs from existing carbon markets because it avoids carbon offsetting entirely.

Our Vision

A world with a safe climate, together with healthy ecosystems, vibrant communities, and reliable clean energy

Our Vision

“Our vision is a world with a safe climate, together with healthy ecosystems, vibrant communities, and reliable clean energy.”

Three Strategic Pillars

1. Resolve

The organization aims to address the climate crisis through incentive-based mechanisms combining “carrot and stick” approaches for climate mitigation. This centers on a novel pricing theory for carbon and proposes carbon rewards to finance climate action and associated benefits.

2. Coordinate

The goal involves aligning governments, markets, and citizens toward collaborative climate management, ecosystem protection, and social advancement. This relies on establishing a global carbon reward marketplace and stakeholder engagement through reward weighting preferences.

Our Mission

Demonstrate and advocate a regenerative financial system for climate action

Our Mission

“Our mission is to demonstrate and advocate a regenerative financial system that can advance a low-carbon transition while promoting ecological health, community wellbeing, and energy reliability.” (Updated January 18, 2025)

Four Core Tasks

The organization commits to completing these objectives:

  1. Establish demonstrations
  2. Manage operations
  3. Advocate the policy
  4. Propose a multilateral governance architecture

Detailed Focus Areas

Demonstrations

The organization prioritizes developing Global Carbon Reward showcases featuring diverse low-carbon technologies through public-private partnerships. These initiatives illustrate how governments can fund climate action without direct taxation and demonstrate how international cost-sharing conflicts can be resolved through a global climate mitigation services market. The demonstrations emphasize stakeholder consensus based on outcome ranking and financial incentives for improved people and planet results.

Our Plan

Advocate and advance the Global Carbon Reward policy through partnerships and demonstrations

Our Plan

Our goal is to advocate and advance the Global Carbon Reward policy with our international partners, by demonstrating the policy’s capacity to incentivise a variety of climate mitigation methods and co-benefits, and the policy’s adaptability in diverse geographic settings.

Three Key Milestones

Partnering

The initial phase focuses on establishing collaborations with recognized NGOs and creating GCR think tanks across multiple countries. These think tanks will facilitate public-private partnerships involving governments, low-carbon technology firms, and nonprofit organizations with sponsorship arrangements.
