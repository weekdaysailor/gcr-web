Connect with the team

We’re actively partnering with philanthropies, investors, governments, and research institutions. Use the options below to find the right point of contact or send us a direct message.

International Coordinator

Ramon Sundblad organizes policy briefings, interviews, and demonstration visits.

Connect with Ramon on LinkedIn to plan speaking engagements or global travel.

Primary Inbox

Email info@globalcarbonreward.org for general questions, policy briefings, or requests from governments and philanthropic partners.

Include your organization, geography, and objective so we can route it quickly.

Donor & Demonstration Support

Need help with contributions or project onboarding? Call our fiscal sponsor, Inquiring Systems Inc., at +1 (707) 939-3900 during Sonoma working hours.

You can also review the instructions on the Donate page.

Specialized contacts

  • Partnerships & demonstrations: Outline the mitigation opportunity, timeline, and stakeholders involved.
  • Press & speaking: Share deadlines, preferred format, and any on-background requests.
  • Research collaborations: Provide links to the relevant publication, data set, or modeling framework.

Contact form

Prefer to use a form? Provide a quick overview of your request and we will route it to the right person.

The contact form is currently disabled. Add a submission endpoint to params.forms.contactEndpoint in hugo.toml to enable it, or email info@globalcarbonreward.org.

Additional notes

  • We respond to most inquiries within two business days.
  • For high-priority policy or investment briefings, mention any deadlines or event dates so we can prioritize appropriately.
  • If you’re looking to collaborate on research or modeling, include relevant publications or datasets in your message.
